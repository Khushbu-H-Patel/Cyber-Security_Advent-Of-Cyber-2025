# YARA Rules - YARA mean one!

This room talks about the basics of YARA, when and why to use, different type of YAR rule. YARA is a tool built to detect, identify and classify malware by searching for unique patterns, behaviour the digital fingerprints left behind by the attackers rather than relying on file names and signatures. YARA beings several advantages: Speed, Flexibility, Control, Share-ability, and Visibility.

The key elements of YARA rule are as below:
**Metadata:** information about the rule itself: who created it, when, and for what purpose?
**Strings:** the clues YARA searches for: text, byte sequences, or regular expressions that mark suspicious content.
**Conditions:** the logic that decides when the rule triggers, combining multiple strings or parameters into a single decision.
